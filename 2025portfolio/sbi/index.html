Context

SBI offers revenue consulting services, and SBI Pro is digital platform for their clients

Prior to starting this platform, I led the redesign of SBI's marketing website for a 4-month project, including web content and branding work. After that, we continued to partner with the client to build SBI Pro, their data and content based platform to add value to their consulting services. This case study focuses on SBI Pro, which after launch empowered clients to visualize their business performance, benchmark KPIs against industry standards, and track their progress towards revenue goals, and allowed SBI to expand their business with this platform as a subscription service.
