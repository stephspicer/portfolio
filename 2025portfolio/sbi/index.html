Iteration: benchmarks & Metrics

A big part of how SBI consultants help execs is showing them which KPIs to focus on. How do we help visualize that?

The consulting relationship centers meetings, manual data collection about the client business, and consultants custom making a lot of slide decks with a lot of charts to share with execs. And then making updated decks throughout the engagement after KPIs change.. etc. So we wanted to explore how to bring that data into the platform in a dynamic way.
