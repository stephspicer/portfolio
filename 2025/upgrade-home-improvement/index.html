From zero to $1 billion in loan originations

B2B2C Product Design, 2022

Began concepting in April 2022 and launched home improvement loan products including a contractor portal, borrower portal, and ops portal as the sole designer for a successful MVP launch in November 2022. Now managing a team of two designers fully supporting the resulting business as it scales

Context

Home improvement loans are different from general personal loans or lines of credit.

The disbursement goes straight to the contractor, not the borrower - in the end this benefits all involved. Home owners get better loan terms because they are inherently lower risk to investors and because the contractor pays a fee for many loan types. Contractors can offer more flexible financing to make more sales.

Planning and Scoping

As a B2B2C project with interacting user types, we came up with the created interconnected journey map to plan our products

During the loan application and payment request process, there is back and forth between contractor and the home owner (borrower). It's important to have clear visibility on each side.

leveraging from existing products

For MVP, we reused what could from other Upgrade products knowing we could improve later

To make the most of resources and launch fast, we focused innovation on differentiating features. This meant basing a lot of the home owner's borrower portal on Upgrade's existing B2C apps
